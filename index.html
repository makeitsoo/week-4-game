Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals in the Game panel. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Current Total score.

You win the game by matching your Total Score to the Random Number and you lose the game if your Total Score goes above the Random Number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from view.

Each time the game restarts, you will be given a new Random Number and crsytal values will change.
